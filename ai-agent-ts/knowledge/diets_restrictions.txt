Common Diets and Dietary Restrictions

Vegetarian:
- Excludes meat and fish; includes eggs and dairy (lacto-ovo)
- Ensure adequate protein: legumes, tofu, tempeh, eggs, dairy, quinoa
- Watch: iron (pair with vitamin C), B12 (supplement or fortified foods), omega-3 (flax, chia, walnuts)

Vegan:
- Excludes all animal products
- Protein: legumes, tofu, tempeh, seitan, edamame
- Key nutrients: B12 (supplement), iron, calcium, vitamin D, omega-3

Gluten-free:
- Required for celiac disease and gluten sensitivity
- Safe grains: rice, quinoa, buckwheat, oats (certified GF), corn
- Read labels; gluten hides in sauces, processed foods

Dairy-free:
- Alternatives: oat milk, almond milk, soy milk (fortified with calcium/vitamin D)
- Calcium from: leafy greens, fortified plant milk, tofu, almonds

Nut allergies:
- Avoid peanuts, tree nuts; check labels and cross-contamination
- Protein alternatives: legumes, seeds (sunflower, pumpkin), soy

Low-carb / Keto:
- Keto: very low carb (<50g), high fat, moderate protein
- May aid short-term weight loss; not suitable for everyone; consult a professional
- Electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium) important on keto

Mediterranean:
- Emphasizes olive oil, fish, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, moderate wine
- Associated with heart health and longevity; flexible and sustainable
